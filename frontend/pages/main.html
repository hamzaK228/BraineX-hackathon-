Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find the right scholarship for me?

+

Our advanced matching algorithm analyzes your academic background, interests, and goals to recommend personalized scholarship opportunities. You can also use our search filters to find scholarships by field, deadline, and program type.

Is BraineX free to use?

+

Yes! BraineX is completely free for students. We believe in democratizing access to education. Premium features for enhanced mentorship and priority support are available for those who want additional benefits.

How does mentor matching work?

+

We match you with mentors based on your field of interest, career goals, and learning style. Our mentors are industry professionals and successful alumni who volunteer their time to guide the next generation.

Can I access scholarships from any country?

+

Absolutely! We feature scholarships from universities and organizations worldwide. Our database includes opportunities for domestic and international students across all continents.

What support do you provide for applications?

+

We provide comprehensive application support including essay review, interview preparation, document guidance, and deadline tracking. Our mentors and resources help you put your best foot forward.

How often is the scholarship database updated?

+

Our scholarship database is updated in real-time through direct API connections with universities and organizations. New opportunities are added daily, and expired ones are automatically removed.
